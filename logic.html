Logic mining

In the MinePro team’s private sector mining consulting journey, they found that almost all facilities face one universal issue. Power. According to the global energy institute, the USA has an average cost is 15.45 cents per kWh, and the global average is 14 cents per kWh. The best industrial rates are generally coming from countries lacking the infrastructure and capacity to even withstand a mining operation.

So how do they solve this problem?

With unique solutions.

In the mining space, private energy is considered the ultimate find. The white whale. Usually guarded behind closed doors and strictly reserved for large companies and enterprise clients, this solution is almost never available for the average retail client. Until now…

This is where Logic Mining comes into play.

In Dylan’s tireless years long global research, and relationship cultivating, he was able to broker a partnership with Canadian private enterprise energy provider Logic Mining.

How It Works

MinePro will be the first tokenized BTC mining project which will reward stakers every single month in BTC. The MINE token will serve as the deflationary governance token over the entire ecosystem. Holders will simply stake their MINE token into the staking pool and earn rewards in BTC every single month. It’s that simple. The staking pool will payout based on time staked (longer stake, larger payout.)

All presalers and airdrop recipients will have the opportunity to stake their token on TGE. If they choose to stake prior to liquidity creation there will be HUGE bonus multipliers on earnings! Remember that as stakers you’ll always have the opportunity to unstake and move throughout the ecosystem. We’re for the people, and support any decision as an investor you have, but we encourage staking because this is the best way to maximize earning in the ecosystem.
