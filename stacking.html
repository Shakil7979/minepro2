Staking

MinePro believes in the power of community. Thats why we're putting the power back in the hands of the people! Our SocialFi campaign will give farmers the ability to earn MASSIVE airdrops for engaging with our socials. Complete tasks and quests to earn points and increase earnings!

Stake to earn years of consistent passive income

MinePro’s revolutionary staking platform allows holders to stake their $MINE token to earn BTC rewards. Stakers will be paid every single month, and profits dropped directly to their wallets. There will be different term lengths for stakers to choose. The longer the stake, the larger the multiplier. Presalers and airdrop recipients will also have the opportunity to stake before launch to earn a huge bonus multiplier.
